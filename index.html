Join us for an exciting event filled with fun and learning!

Get ready to soak up the sun and celebrate in style at the Summer Time Bash 2025—an unforgettable all-day extravaganza packed with music, food, and fun! Hosted at the scenic Lakeside Park, this annual community event brings the heat with live DJ sets, local band performances, and a vibrant market featuring handmade goods and summer treats. Families can enjoy inflatable obstacle courses, face painting, and lawn games, while food trucks line the boardwalk with everything from gourmet burgers to shaved ice. Whether you're here to dance, dine, or just unwind in the sun, there's something for everyone.


This year’s bash will also feature exciting special events you won’t want to miss. Join the midday Water Balloon Showdown—a massive community water fight for all ages—and cheer on contestants in the wildly entertaining Pie-Eating Contest. As the sun begins to set, gather for the Firefly Glow Parade, where guests can decorate lanterns and walk together through the illuminated park. Finally, cap off the night with a dazzling lakeside fireworks show set to summer anthems. Don’t forget your beach blankets and sunglasses—this summer bash is the ultimate celebration of sunshine and good vibes!
